✨ Huling Barang ✨

Adella Mae, ang aking mortal na kaaway.
Pards — salamat sa unexpected friendship. You made this whole internship oddly fun, frustrating, challenging, and somehow... one for the books. You annoyed the hell out of me — and yet, I kept wanting to talk to you. Fight with you. Win against you (kahit talo ako most of the time). You never back down, and I hate how much I respect that. Tawag ko sa’yo mambabarang kasi parang may sumpa kang bitbit — hindi ko alam kung bakit kapag ikaw ang kaharap ko, gusto ko makipag-debate, pero gusto ko rin makinig. My best memory with you is yung nag 1v1 na usap tayo sa pantry, di ko alam kung tanda mo pa yun pero I personally enjoyed that conversation, 1st and last na maayos na usap na pala natin yun, because the rest is pang-aapi mo na sakin. Nakakainis, pero aminin na natin, may pagka-charming ‘yung asar mo. I admire how you carry yourself — confident but not arrogant, witty without trying, and always with something sharp to say. Pasikat ka boi hagshgashasg, pero aminado akong nakakabilib ka. You know when to lead, when to shut things down, and when to shut me up — and somehow, I don’t even mind. There were times I felt a gap between us. Maybe imagined, maybe real — but it made me curious. And that curiosity turned into admiration. You became a person I truly value, even if we spent most of our time lowkey competing or roasting each other for sport. Despite the short time, I saw something real in you — someone strong, capable, and bound to do great things. You have this quiet fire that makes people take notice, even if you pretend you don’t care. And that’s what makes you unforgettable. So good luck — sa research kemerut nyo, sa buhay-buhay, sa lahat ng susunod na laban. And pards, if you ever feel like trip mo manira ng tao, mambarang or manira ng mood, just message me. I’ll be waiting — armed with a comeback, and probably… coffee.

Wala naman ako pinagkaiba kay Mommy D. at Manny P. may gusto din naman ako sayo, nakakainis.

I appreciate you. I’m proud of you. And lab na lab ko kayo kahit mga tangahin kayo. This is goodbye for now, Pards. Don’t be a stranger. Pakyu. (Pero pakyu na may pagmamahal.)
